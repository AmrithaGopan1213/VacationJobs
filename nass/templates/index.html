PROCEDURE

STEPS TO JOIN AND WORK AS A SOCIAL MEDIA PROMOTER

How to Join

  1. Creating multiple accounts is not permitted. Any duplicate accounts will be blocked, and all generated income will be forfeited.
  2. Click on the website link you received and navigate to the Affiliate section.
  3. Click the "Register" button to create your account.
  4. Fill in the required details including username and password. Paste your Affiliate link and click the register button.
  5. A verification link will be sent to your email.
  6. Open your email and click the verification link to confirm your account.
  7. Log in using your username and password.
  8. Click on "My Profile" in your account dashboard.
  9. Scroll down to find "Your Referral Link".
  10. Copy this referral link.
  11. Share the link with your friends.
  12. Explain these steps to them as well to help them join.

💰 Turn Every Task Into Real Cash!

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to "Earnings" and click on "My Tasks".
  3. Review the list of available tasks assigned to you.
  4. Click "Claim Task" and confirm your claim (Note: Each task can only be claimed once).
  5. After completing the task, click "Submit Proof".
  6. Access the task link provided in the details.
  7. Complete the work as described in the task requirements and collect proof of completion.
  8. Upload your proof and submit it for review.
  9. Wait for approval (processing may take up to one business hour).
  10. Once approved, go to "Earnings" and click "Withdrawal".
  11. Your payment for completed tasks will be credited to your wallet balance.

FOLLOW THESE STEPS STRICTLY, ANY ERROR MAY CAUSE LOSE OF INCOME, AND WE ARE NOT LIABLE FOR ANY LOSS.
